Fibration Surface in Stereographic Projection

  • We are looking for two families of curves: blue and orange.

  • We call where blue and orange curves coincide a rung.

  • Any part of the curves that is not a rung is called a fibration boundary.

  • We find our curves so that the fibration boundary is exactly the trefoil knot.
